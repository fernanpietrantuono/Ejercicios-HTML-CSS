French Fries

Ingredients:

  • 3 or 4 potatoes (300 gr)
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Peel the potatoes.
  2. Chop them into sticks.

If you like the potatoes fried in hot oil, follow the next steps:

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan.
  2. Cook the potatoes until they are golden and crunchy.
  3. Remove from the oil and add salt, ketchup or cheddar sauce as desire.

If you like healthy french fries, follow the next steps:

  1. Pre-heat the air fryer for 5 minutes to 200Â° C before to cook.
  2. Cook the potatoes between 18 and 22 minutes.
  3. Put the french fries on a serving plate and add salt, ketchup or cheddar sauce as desire.
